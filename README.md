## Project Logo

![Canvasphere Logo]

This logo represents the **Canvasphere** concept — a vibrant, creative space where ideas orbit around a central core of design and collaboration.

- **Sphere** – Symbolizes unity, inclusivity, and the global reach of creative work.
- **Orbiting Trails** – Represent collaboration, motion, and the continuous flow of ideas.
- **Gradient Colors** – Transition from deep blue to vivid magenta, reflecting creativity, innovation, and imagination.
- **Minimal Background** – Highlights the focus on content, keeping distractions out of the workspace.
